About Gokulam Constructions

Gokulam Constructions is a reputed construction company established in 2025, based in Chilakaluripeta, Andhra Pradesh. Over the years, we have delivered premium residential projects, combining modern architecture with quality construction.

We specialize in building spacious 2BHK and 3BHK apartments as well as individual luxury homes. Our projects are designed to offer comfort, elegance, and functionality, ensuring a perfect living experience for families.

Today, Gokulam Constructions operates across multiple regions in Andhra Pradesh including Guntur, Vijayawada, and Tirupati, bringing excellence and reliability to every project we undertake.
